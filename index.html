Based in Ottawa, Ontario, Coco's Chocolatiers is proudly Canadian. The Cococo team are expert manufacturers and retailers of award-winning confectionary chocolates, and also are a group of passionate and committed people, who care greatly about the customers experience. Cococo is dedicated to improving lives, using only chocolate made with Rainforest Alliance Certified® cocoa and cocoa butter to promote a sustainable chocolate future, and benefits the farmers, communities, and ecosystems involved with cocoa production in West Africa.
